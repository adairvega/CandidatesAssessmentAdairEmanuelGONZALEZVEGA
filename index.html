Excercise 4

Complete below statement.

a. Write an SQL statement that lists worker first name, last name and department name for those workers that are over 30 years old and their department is not Taxes.

b. Write an SQL statement that lists the number of enrolled workers on each department (excluding Security) and the Annual Expenses value per department that is the sum of annual salary of all enrolled workers.

My result:

a:

SELECT w.firstName, w.lastName, d.departmentName

FROM workers w JOIN departments d ON w.departmentId = d.departmentId

WHERE w.age > 30 AND d.departmentName NOT LIKE "Taxes";

b:

SELECT d.departmentName, COUNT(w.*) as workersEnrrolled, COUNT(w.monthlySalary)

FROM workers w INNER JOIN departments d

ON w.departmentId = d.departmentId WHERE d.departmentName NOT LIKE "Security"

GROUP BY d.departmentName;
